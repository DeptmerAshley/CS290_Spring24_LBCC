Nightmare of Mensis

The Nightmare of Mensis is one of the most harrowing areas in Bloodborne, representing the twisted results of the School of Mensis's eldritch experiments. This nightmare realm is filled with horrifying creatures, deadly traps, and profound lore that reveals the darkest secrets of the game's world.

The History of the Nightmare of Mensis

The Nightmare of Mensis is the result of the School of Mensis's attempts to commune with the Great Ones. Their experiments created a twisted nightmare realm where reality and madness intertwine, trapping the scholars and their subjects in an endless cycle of horror.

The School of Mensis, led by Micolash, sought to achieve enlightenment by contacting the Great Ones through the use of the Old Blood and arcane rituals. Their ambition led to the creation of the Nightmare of Mensis, a nightmarish dimension where the boundaries between reality and nightmare are blurred. The realm is filled with grotesque creatures and eldritch horrors, manifestations of the scholars' failed experiments and the wrath of the Great Ones.

Bosses

The Nightmare of Mensis features some of the most challenging and memorable bosses in Bloodborne, each representing the culmination of the School of Mensis's dark experiments.

Micolash, Host of the Nightmare

Micolash is the primary antagonist of the Nightmare of Mensis. As the leader of the School of Mensis, he guides the player through a twisted maze of traps and illusions. The fight against Micolash is a battle of wits, requiring players to navigate his labyrinth and confront his dark powers.

Mergo's Wet Nurse

Mergo's Wet Nurse is the final boss of the Nightmare of Mensis. This eldritch creature is tasked with protecting the infant Great One, Mergo. The battle against Mergo's Wet Nurse is intense and surreal, testing the player's skills and resolve.

Lore

The Nightmare of Mensis is rich with lore, revealing the dark ambitions of the School of Mensis and their attempts to commune with the Great Ones. Exploring this area provides insights into the tragic consequences of their experiments and the true nature of the Great Ones.

The School of Mensis

The School of Mensis is an elite faction within the Healing Church, dedicated to the study of the Great Ones and the pursuit of enlightenment through arcane rituals. Their experiments with the Old Blood and their attempts to contact the Great Ones led to the creation of the Nightmare of Mensis.

The Experiments

The scholars of the School of Mensis conducted numerous experiments to commune with the Great Ones, using the Old Blood and arcane rituals. These experiments often resulted in horrific transformations and the creation of eldritch abominations. The Nightmare of Mensis is a testament to their ambition and the consequences of their hubris.

Key Figures

Micolash, Host of the Nightmare: Micolash is the leader of the School of Mensis and the architect of the Nightmare of Mensis. His obsession with the Great Ones and his descent into madness are central to the lore of this area.

Mergo: Mergo is an infant Great One, whose presence in the Nightmare of Mensis is shrouded in mystery. The protection of Mergo by the Wet Nurse highlights the Great Ones' influence over the realm.

Sidequests

Mensis Cage

The Mensis Cage is a key item found in the Nightmare of Mensis. It serves as a symbol of the scholars' attempts to commune with the Great Ones and the madness that ensued.

First Interaction: The Mensis Cage is found on the corpse of a Mensis scholar. Obtaining it requires players to explore the depths of the nightmare and confront its horrors.

Subsequent Interactions: Using the Mensis Cage unlocks new areas and provides access to secret locations within the Nightmare of Mensis.

Final Interaction: The Mensis Cage reveals more about the scholars' experiments and their ultimate fate.

The Brain of Mensis

The Brain of Mensis is a grotesque entity found in the Nightmare of Mensis. It is the result of the scholars' failed experiments and serves as a source of insight and horror.

First Interaction: The Brain of Mensis is encountered in a hidden area of the nightmare. Players must navigate treacherous paths and avoid deadly traps to reach it.

Subsequent Interactions: Interacting with the Brain of Mensis provides valuable items and insights into the nature of the nightmare and the scholars' experiments.

Final Interaction: The Brain of Mensis's fate is tied to the player's actions, offering different outcomes and rewards based on their choices.
