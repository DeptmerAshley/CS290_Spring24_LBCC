Old Yharnam

Old Yharnam is a desolate and abandoned district in Bloodborne, shrouded in fog and plagued by beasts. Once a thriving part of the city, it was burned down and quarantined to contain the spread of the beastly scourge.

The History of Old Yharnam

Old Yharnam was once a prosperous district, but it fell victim to a terrible outbreak of the beast plague. In a desperate attempt to control the infection, the Healing Church decided to burn the entire area, sealing it off from the rest of Yharnam. The ruins of Old Yharnam stand as a grim reminder of the city's dark past.

The Healing Church's decision to burn Old Yharnam was controversial, but it was deemed necessary to prevent the plague from spreading further. The area is now overrun with beasts and is avoided by most of Yharnam's inhabitants. Hunters, however, venture into Old Yharnam to uncover its secrets and combat the lingering threats.

Bosses

Old Yharnam features some of the game's most intense and challenging bosses.

Blood-starved Beast

The Blood-starved Beast is the primary boss of Old Yharnam. This grotesque creature is known for its ferocity and the toxic environment it creates during the battle. Defeating it is a significant achievement for any hunter.

Djura, the Retired Hunter

Djura is an optional boss in Old Yharnam. He is a retired hunter who has taken it upon himself to protect the beasts of the area, believing them to be victims rather than enemies. Confronting Djura presents players with a moral dilemma and a challenging fight.

Lore

Old Yharnam is rich with lore, offering insights into the early days of the Healing Church and the beast plague. The area is filled with remnants of the past, telling a story of desperation and sacrifice.

The Healing Church's Role

The Healing Church played a central role in the downfall of Old Yharnam. Their experiments with the Old Blood and subsequent attempts to contain the beast plague had devastating effects. The decision to burn Old Yharnam was a drastic measure that highlights the church's desperate efforts to control the situation.

The Spread of the Beast Plague

The beast plague spread rapidly through Old Yharnam, turning its inhabitants into feral creatures. The Healing Church's response was swift and brutal, but ultimately, it could not save the district from destruction. The abandoned buildings and lingering beasts serve as a testament to the plague's impact.

Key Figures

Djura, the Retired Hunter: Djura is a key figure in Old Yharnam, representing a different perspective on the beasts. He sees them as victims of the plague, deserving of protection rather than extermination. His presence adds complexity to the lore of Old Yharnam.

The Blood-starved Beast: The Blood-starved Beast is a manifestation of the plague's horrors. Its grotesque appearance and deadly nature embody the consequences of the Healing Church's experiments.

Sidequests

Djura's Quest

Djura's quest is a significant side story in Old Yharnam. Interacting with him and understanding his perspective on the beasts provides a deeper understanding of the game's lore.

First Interaction: Djura warns players to stay away from Old Yharnam, expressing his desire to protect the beasts.

Subsequent Interactions: Depending on the player's actions, Djura can become a formidable ally or a challenging opponent.

Final Interaction: Djura's fate is determined by the player's choices, influencing the narrative of Old Yharnam.

The Powder Kegs

The Powder Kegs are a group of hunters associated with Old Yharnam. Their story provides additional context to the events that transpired in the district.

First Interaction: Players learn about the Powder Kegs through various items and environmental clues in Old Yharnam.

Subsequent Interactions: The history and impact of the Powder Kegs are revealed through exploration and combat encounters.

Final Interaction: The legacy of the Powder Kegs is tied to the fate of Old Yharnam, offering players a glimpse into the past.
