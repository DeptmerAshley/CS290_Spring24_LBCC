Upper Cathedral Ward

The Upper Cathedral Ward is a secretive and eerie area in Bloodborne, known for its deep connection to the Healing Church and its eldritch experiments. This area is a key location for uncovering the dark secrets of the church and its dealings with the Great Ones.

The History of Upper Cathedral Ward

The Upper Cathedral Ward is a secluded part of Yharnam, accessible only through a locked gate. It houses the highest echelons of the Healing Church, where secret experiments were conducted to commune with the Great Ones and harness their power.

The Upper Cathedral Ward was once the pinnacle of the Healing Church's power, where scholars and clerics conducted their most profound and secretive experiments. The area is now abandoned and overrun by the horrors that were unleashed by their attempts to commune with the Great Ones. The ward's dark history is evident in its haunting atmosphere and the eldritch abominations that inhabit it.

Bosses

The Upper Cathedral Ward features some of the most chilling bosses in Bloodborne, each embodying the twisted results of the Healing Church's experiments.

Celestial Emissary

The Celestial Emissary is one of the primary bosses of the Upper Cathedral Ward. This being is the result of the Healing Church's attempts to transform humans into celestial beings. The fight against the Celestial Emissary is both surreal and challenging.

Ebritas, Daughter of the Cosmos

Ebritas is the final boss of the Upper Cathedral Ward and one of the Great Ones that the Healing Church sought to commune with. This battle is one of the most difficult in the game, testing the player's skill and resolve.

Lore

The Upper Cathedral Ward is rich with lore, revealing the Healing Church's deepest secrets and its relationship with the Great Ones. Exploring this area provides insights into the church's most ambitious and dangerous experiments.

The Healing Church's Experiments

The Healing Church's scholars and clerics conducted numerous experiments in the Upper Cathedral Ward in an attempt to commune with the Great Ones. These experiments involved the use of the Old Blood and various arcane rituals. The results were often disastrous, leading to the creation of horrific creatures and the corruption of the church itself.

The Celestial Emissaries

The Celestial Emissaries are the products of the Healing Church's attempts to ascend humanity to a higher plane of existence. These beings are a testament to the church's ambition and its willingness to sacrifice anything in the pursuit of knowledge and power.

Key Figures

Ebritas, Daughter of the Cosmos: Ebritas is a Great One discovered by the Healing Church. The church's attempts to communicate with Ebritas led to many of the horrors found in the Upper Cathedral Ward. She remains a central figure in the church's lore and its pursuit of enlightenment.

The Choir: The Choir is the upper echelon of the Healing Church, consisting of scholars who dedicated themselves to understanding and communing with the Great Ones. Their experiments and discoveries are crucial to the lore of the Upper Cathedral Ward.

Sidequests

The Orphanage Key

The Orphanage Key is a significant item found in the Upper Cathedral Ward. It unlocks the door to the Orphanage, where players can uncover more about the Healing Church's experiments and the fate of the children taken there.

First Interaction: The Orphanage Key is found in a hidden area within the Upper Cathedral Ward. Obtaining it requires players to explore thoroughly and defeat challenging enemies.

Subsequent Interactions: Using the Orphanage Key opens up new areas for exploration, revealing more about the church's dark practices and the experiments conducted on the orphans.

Final Interaction: The discoveries made in the Orphanage provide crucial insights into the lore of the Healing Church and its ultimate goals.

The Choir

The Choir is an elite group within the Healing Church, responsible for many of the experiments conducted in the Upper Cathedral Ward. Interacting with members of the Choir provides players with deeper understanding of the church's objectives and methods.

First Interaction: Players encounter members of the Choir throughout the Upper Cathedral Ward, each offering valuable information and items related to the church's experiments.

Subsequent Interactions: The Choir members often guard important locations and items, requiring players to defeat them to progress further into the Ward.

Final Interaction: The defeat of the Choir members and the exploration of their quarters reveal the extent of the church's ambitions and the true nature of their experiments.
