Overview

Bloodborne is an action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Sony Computer Entertainment. Released in 2015, the game is set in the gothic, Victorian-era inspired city of Yharnam, where players take on the role of a Hunter tasked with uncovering the city's dark secrets.

Key Features

  • Fast-paced, challenging combat
  • Deep lore and intricate storytelling
  • Gothic, atmospheric world design
  • Extensive exploration and discovery
  • Unique Insight mechanic
  • Procedurally generated Chalice Dungeons
  • Customization of Hunter's appearance and abilities
