Forbidden Woods

The Forbidden Woods is one of the most treacherous areas in Bloodborne, filled with deadly traps, hostile villagers, and dangerous creatures. This area is a vast, dense forest that hides many secrets and serves as a gateway to Byrgenwerth.

The History of the Forbidden Woods

The Forbidden Woods have long been shrouded in mystery and fear. This dense forest is avoided by the citizens of Yharnam due to the dangerous creatures that dwell within and the numerous traps set by the hostile inhabitants.

The Forbidden Woods were once a boundary to protect the secrets of Byrgenwerth and the Healing Church. The dense forest and the winding paths are filled with traps, making it difficult for any outsider to navigate. The woods are inhabited by villagers who have become hostile and deranged, attacking any intruder who dares to enter their territory.

Bosses

The Forbidden Woods features some of the most challenging bosses in Bloodborne, each requiring skill and strategy to defeat.

Shadow of Yharnam

The Shadow of Yharnam is a trio of powerful enemies encountered in the Forbidden Woods. Each shadow has unique abilities and attacks, making this boss fight a test of the player's skill and adaptability.

Snake Ball

The Snake Ball is a unique miniboss found in the Forbidden Woods. These grotesque masses of writhing serpents are deadly and can overwhelm players with their sheer numbers and speed.

Lore

The Forbidden Woods are rich with lore, offering insights into the secrets of Byrgenwerth and the early days of the Healing Church. The area is filled with clues about the dark experiments conducted in Byrgenwerth and the consequences of those actions.

Byrgenwerth

Byrgenwerth is a key location in Bloodborne's lore, and the Forbidden Woods serve as a gateway to this mysterious institution. Byrgenwerth was founded by Master Willem, who conducted numerous arcane experiments in pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment. The secrets of Byrgenwerth are closely guarded, and the Forbidden Woods act as a natural barrier to protect these secrets.

The Experiments

The experiments conducted in Byrgenwerth involved the use of the Old Blood and attempts to commune with the Great Ones. These experiments had far-reaching consequences, leading to the spread of the beast plague and the eventual downfall of Yharnam. The Forbidden Woods are littered with evidence of these dark practices.

Key Figures

Master Willem: Master Willem is the founder of Byrgenwerth and a key figure in Bloodborne's lore. His pursuit of knowledge led to the discovery of the Old Blood and the subsequent experiments that had catastrophic results.

The Shadows of Yharnam: The Shadows of Yharnam are mysterious figures connected to Byrgenwerth. Their presence in the Forbidden Woods suggests a deeper connection to the institution's dark secrets.

Sidequests

Valtr, Master of the League

Valtr is a significant NPC found in the Forbidden Woods. He is the leader of the League, a group dedicated to hunting down the vile creatures of Yharnam. Valtr offers players the opportunity to join the League and participate in their hunts.

First Interaction: Valtr can be found in a shack in the Forbidden Woods. He invites the player to join the League and offers the League Oath gesture.

Subsequent Interactions: As a member of the League, players can earn rewards by completing hunts and defeating enemies marked by the League's Vileblood Register.

Final Interaction: Valtr's questline culminates in a confrontation with the Vermin, creatures that the League seeks to eradicate. Players who prove their dedication to the League are rewarded with unique items and gestures.

The Cannibal Villagers

The Forbidden Woods are home to a group of deranged villagers who have resorted to cannibalism. These villagers are hostile to outsiders and pose a significant threat to anyone who ventures into their territory.

First Interaction: Players encounter the cannibal villagers throughout the Forbidden Woods. These enemies are aggressive and often ambush unsuspecting travelers.

Subsequent Interactions: The cannibal villagers drop unique items and offer a glimpse into the madness that has taken hold of the inhabitants of the Forbidden Woods.

Final Interaction: Defeating the cannibal villagers and uncovering their dark practices provides players with valuable resources and insights into the area's lore.
